# Privacy Policy for Find MyCar

Last updated: February 24, 2026

The **Find MyCar** app is built as a Commercial app. This SERVICE is provided by the developer at no cost and is intended for use as is.

### 1. Data Collection and Use
We believe in your right to privacy. **Find MyCar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** * We do not track your location history on our servers.
* We do not collect device identifiers.
* No personal information (name, email, etc.) is required to use the app.

### 2. Location Services
To help you find your vehicle, the app may request access to your device's Location Services. This data is processed **locally on your device only** to pin your car's position. This information is never shared with the developer or any third parties.

### 3. Third-Party Services
The app does not use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### 4. Security
Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data stored on our servers to protect. However, we recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric lock to protect the information stored locally.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
